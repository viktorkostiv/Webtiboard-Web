E-Commerce module

WooCommerce integration — every tab explained

8 dedicated tabs covering every aspect of your store, all synced in real time.

Products

Full product table with inline editing, image preview, SKU, price, stock, categories, tags and status. Bulk actions, column sort, search and filter by category or status.

Orders

View all orders with customer name, total, item count, date, and payment/fulfillment status. Filter by order status (pending, processing, completed, cancelled, refunded).

Statistics

Revenue charts (bar, line, pie), completed vs pending breakdown, top products by sales, average order value, daily/monthly views. Multiple chart types selectable.

Import / Export

Bulk import products from Excel with smart column mapping. Export current product list to CSV. Sync status shows created / updated / skipped counts after each import.

Future Products

Schedule products to be published at a future date. See upcoming releases in a dedicated list, separate from live inventory. Plan launches without affecting current store.

Trash

Deleted products are moved to trash, not permanently removed. Restore single products or bulk-restore. Permanent delete available from trash view only.

Low Stock Alerts

Automatically highlights products below your defined stock threshold. Separate tab dedicated to low-stock items so nothing falls through the cracks.

Activity Log

Every product change, sync, import, and price edit is logged here. Know exactly who changed what price, when products were synced, and what was imported.
